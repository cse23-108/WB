Sarmien

Synopsis

"Sarmien" is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of a troubled detective, John Sarmien, as he delves into a series of grisly murders haunting his city. Tormented by his past and struggling with addiction, Sarmien must navigate a web of lies, deceit, and personal demons to uncover the truth behind the killings.

Plot

As the bodies pile up and pressure mounts from both the media and his superiors, Sarmien finds himself drawn into a twisted game of cat and mouse with a cunning serial killer. With each clue leading to dead ends and each suspect proving more elusive than the last, Sarmien must confront his own inner darkness to catch a killer before it's too late.

Themes

"Sarmien" explores themes of redemption, obsession, and the thin line between justice and vengeance. Through its tense atmosphere, complex characters, and shocking twists, the film offers a chilling portrayal of the human psyche and the lengths we will go to in pursuit of truth.
